Panch Kedar Trek 2025

12 Days / 11 Nights | Moderate Trek | Starts & Ends at Rishikesh

Explore the five holy Kedars – Kedarnath, Madhyamaheshwar, Tungnath, Rudranath, Kalpeshwar

Itinerary Overview

  • Day 1: Rishikesh to Guptkashi (180 km / 6–7 hrs)
  • Day 2: Guptkashi → Sonprayag → Kedarnath (30 km + 20 km trek)
  • Day 3: Kedarnath → Sonprayag → Guptkashi
  • Day 4: Guptkashi → Ransi → Upper Bantoli (Drive + 6 km trek)
  • Day 5: Trek to Madhyamaheshwar (11 km)
  • Day 6: Return to Ransi (17 km)
  • Day 7: Ransi to Chopta (50 km)
  • Day 8: Chopta → Tungnath → Sagar (10 km trek + 40 km drive)
  • Day 9: Trek to Lyuti (10 km)
  • Day 10: Trek to Rudranath & return to Lyuti (18 km)
  • Day 11: Lyuti → Sagar → Kalpeshwar
  • Day 12: Kalpeshwar → Rishikesh (250 km / 8–9 hrs)

Inclusions

  • All transport from Rishikesh to Rishikesh
  • All meals from Day 1 Dinner to Day 12 Breakfast
  • Accommodation (Hotel/Camps/HomeStay – triple sharing)
  • Certified trek leader, guide, cook & support staff
  • Trek equipment: tents, sleeping bags, utensils, etc.
  • First aid, oxygen cylinders & stretchers
  • Forest entry, tolls & permit fees

Exclusions

  • Meals during transfer days
  • Any emergency evacuation costs
  • Personal expenses (shopping, tips, etc.)
  • Anything not mentioned in inclusions
