Chopta Tungnath With Deorital (3 Days / 2 Nights)

Explore one of the most serene and spiritually significant treks in the Garhwal Himalayas. The Tungnath and Chandrashilla trek offers not just a physical journey but also a spiritual awakening, surrounded by breathtaking views and tranquil forests.

Itinerary

  • Arrive at Chopta, a scenic Himalayan village
  • Check-in to camp/hotel and freshen up
  • Explore local sights and acclimatize
  • Enjoy dinner and overnight stay at the campsite

  • Early morning start for the trek
  • Trek through rhododendron and pine forests
  • Visit the sacred Tungnath Temple
  • Continue to Chandrashilla Peak for panoramic Himalayan views
  • Descend and camp near Tungnath
  • Warm dinner and overnight stay at the campsite

  • Morning check-out from the camp
  • Short trek (2.5 km) to the serene Deoriyatal lake
  • Trek through lush green trails
  • Depart for onward journey after the trek

What's Included

  • Accommodation in camps at Chopta
  • Delicious vegetarian meals and refreshments
  • Guided trek to Tungnath, Chandrashilla & Deoriyatal
  • Transportation from Haridwar/Rishikesh/Delhi (Pickup & Drop)
  • First aid kit and emergency assistance

Cost

  • ₹5,999 per person (Double Sharing)
  • ₹6,999 per person (Single Occupancy)

Booking & Cancellation Policy

  • Booking: ₹3000 per person advance to confirm
  • Cancellation: 30% fee if cancelled within 3 days of trek date
