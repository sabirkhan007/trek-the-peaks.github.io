Kedarkantha Trek (5 Days / 4 Nights)

Embark on the scenic Kedarkantha Trek and experience the beauty of the snow-capped Himalayas. This 5-day journey takes you through stunning landscapes, tranquil forests, and a picturesque base camp at Sankri.

Quick View
  • Best Time To Visit: November to April
  • Temperature: Day: 7°C to 13°C | Night: -4°C to 3°C
  • Kedarkantha Trek Distance: 20 km (Maximum)
  • Altitude: 12,500 ft
  • Difficulty Level: Easy to Moderate
  • Railway Station: Dehradun & Haridwar are the nearest
  • Airport: Jolly Grant Airport, Dehradun
  • Base Camp: Sankri (10 hrs Drive from Dehradun)

Itinerary

  • Begin your Kedarkantha trek by driving from Dehradun to Sankri early in the morning, at 6:30 AM.
  • This 210 km drive offers scenic views, passing through the picturesque Garhwal region, twisting roads, and charming local villages such as Mussoorie and Naitwar.
  • Upon reaching Sankri village, you’ll set up camp in this quaint village, approximately 13 km inside the Govind National Park.

  • Start early in the morning for a trek from Sankri to Juda ka Talaab, located at an altitude of 9,100 feet.
  • The trek offers stunning views of the Sankri village and the surrounding landscape.
  • Upon arrival at Juda ka Talaab, enjoy the serene beauty of the pond, which is surrounded by pine and oak trees. The calm atmosphere here sets the perfect tone for a peaceful overnight stay.

  • Embark on a trek from Juda ka Talaab to the Kedarkantha base camp, situated at an altitude of 11,250 feet.
  • The trek passes through stunning pine forests, and during the winter, the lake at Juda ka Talaab freezes, offering a unique chance to walk on ice and enjoy the slopes.
  • Reach the Kedarkantha base camp after a 4 km trek (approximately 2 hours) and set up camp in the midst of meadows surrounded by pine and oak trees.
  • Relax under the starlit sky and enjoy the tranquil environment, erasing any fatigue accumulated during the journey.

  • Today is the most exciting day of the trek. We begin the ascent towards the Kedarkantha peak, a steep climb that will take you through dense forests and offer stunning views of the surrounding valleys.
  • Along the way, you’ll encounter breathtaking mountain ranges, including the majestic Dhauladhar range, Chainsheel Pass, Har Ki Dun valley, and Rupin valley.
  • Other notable peaks visible on the trek include Swargarohini, Black Peak, Bandarpoonch, Gangotri, Yamnotri, Jorkanden, and Draupadi ka Danda.
  • Upon reaching the summit, you’ll take some time to relax and enjoy panoramic views of the entire region before descending back to the Kedarkantha base camp.
  • After taking a well-deserved break, continue descending towards Hargoan, where you will stay overnight.

  • On the final day of the trek, descend from Juda ka Talaab to Sankri.
  • Once at Sankri, drive back to Dehradun, taking in the majestic views of the Himalayas along the way.
  • The snow-clad peaks will make the journey even more beautiful as you witness the rising sun casting its golden rays over the pine trees and meadows.
  • Before concluding the trek, stop to admire the views of Har Ki Doon and its stunning meadows, a perfect spot for a few more photographs.
